## Misc

| English   |      Kannada
|----------|-------------|
| To Me        |   Nanage       
| To You        |   Ninage / Nimage     
| To him/her/them       |   Avarige  

<br/><br/>


**Reject** - _Bēḍa_ (E* - should be enunciated with a lengthy é as in résumé, da* as done 'do')<br/>
**say plain 'no' /disagree** - _illa_
**say 'not that'/deny** - _alla_

**Agree** - _houdu_ (hou* - should be enunciated like howl 'how', and d* as the 'th')<br/>
**say plain 'yes'** - _hunnn_ <br/>
**say 'correct'** - _sari / Sariyāda_ <br/>

## Conversational sentences
| English               |      Kannada
|----------             |-------------
I am an Engineer.       | Naanu Engineer.
My name is Anita.       | Nanna hesaru Anita.
What is your name?      | Nimma hesaru yenu?
What do you do?         | Neevu yenu madtira?
Which is your place?    | Nimma ooru yaavudu?
My place is Bangalore.  | Nanna ooru Bangalore.
Where do you stay?      | Neevu yelli irthira?
I stay in Koramangala.  | Naanu Koramangala-dalli irthini.
Who is she/he?          | Avaru yaaru?
He is my friend.        | Avaru nanna geleya.
I know kannada          | Nanage kannaḍa gottu
I don't know Kannada    | Nanage kannaḍa gottilla.
I want that             | Nanage adu bēku
